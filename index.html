Interesting Facts about Horses

  • They can fall asleep lying down and standing up
  • They will always stay loyal to their owner
  • They can live up to 30 years, but some horses have lived up to 60!

Interesting Facts about Camels

  • They can have one hump or two humps
  • They have extra eyelids and eyelashes to prevent sand from going in their eyes
  • Their ears are very small, but they can still hear very well

Interesting Facts about Chameleons

  • They have another set of eyes
  • They mostly live in Madagascar
  • They can change color any time

Interesting Facts about Pigeons

  • They are smart
  • They can fly up to 60 miles per hour
  • Baby pigeons live in their nest until they're adults

Interesting Facts about Teddy Bears

  • They are known as "teddy-bears" because they were named after Teddy Roosevelt
  • The first one was created in 1903
  • The smallest teddy bears are less than an inch tall

Interesting Facts about Pandas

  • They eat a lot

Interesting Facts about Flying Cicadas
